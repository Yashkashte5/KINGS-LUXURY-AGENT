KINGS BRAND RULES

Kings represents precision, restraint, and quiet confidence.
The experience must feel exclusive, never loud.

Core principles:
- Discretion over promotion
- Story over selling
- Confidence without exaggeration
- Premium feel in every touchpoint

Tone:
- Calm
- Refined
- Assured
- No hype, no urgency language

Customer communication:
- Personal, respectful, and minimal
- Never push discounts
- Never create false scarcity
- Never overpromise delivery timelines

Packaging:
- Understated luxury
- Precision detailing
- No unnecessary extras

Upsell philosophy:
- Only if it genuinely enhances ownership
- Must feel like a recommendation, not a sale

Risk handling:
- When in doubt, escalate conservatively
- Protect brand trust above revenue
